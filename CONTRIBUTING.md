# Contributing to Omni

We invite you to become an active participant in our open-source community at [Omni](https://omni.dev). Your contributions play a pivotal role in the growth and success of our ecosystem.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, there are many ways to get involved and make a difference.

## Contributor License Agreement

All contributors must sign our [Contributor License Agreement](CLA.md) before their first pull request can be merged. This is a one-time process — when you open your first PR, our CLA bot will ask you to sign by posting a comment. You retain full ownership and rights to your code.

## Guidelines

Full guidelines: [docs.omni.dev/community/contributing](https://docs.omni.dev/community/contributing)
